The Verdict

‘Today's surfboard nose guards are designed to protect the board, not the surfer. The Blunt changes all that. This is the first surfboard eye guard designed from beginning to end thinking about the safety of the surfer and others around him or her.’

The Comparison

This is the the future of noseguards. The blunt is not a simple piece of plastic. It has been engineered to perfection in order to add a series of protective features that will help the enjoyment of surfing last.

Clearly, the traditional nose guards have one evident flaw, they still end in a spear shaped tip. Basis knowledge simply told us that that was not a good idea. But, we wanted to scientifically prove that a blunt tip is the right way to go when designing for surfing eye guards... and we did!

He understands the latent dangers and has personally experienced some of them. He is on a mission to develop high quality protective gear for extreme sports, The Blunt is his initial project in this venture.
